our story

slakka was founded by Kunal Lohia as a way to experiment on business ideas with the family and either establish extra income streams or learn by trying. Entrepreneurship has always been in the blood but ideas never really got off the ground… until agentic AI came along. We see this as a total game-changer that can have profound implications for the world as big as the internet did. So what better way to get your head around this exploding landscape than by trying the technologies yourself. So we have setup OpenClaw at home and are trying different LLMs to uncover the art of the possible.
