slakka mobile product concept

small mobile apps that solve one real thing well.

kapp is being shaped as a lightweight family of focused mobile apps, starting with simple tools for routines, lists, reminders, and small everyday coordination. The MVP direction is deliberately narrow, useful, and fast to launch.

review-ready concept

first release idea

Lead with a polished utility app instead of a broad platform pitch.

  • Core promise: quick setup, clean reminders, minimal friction.
  • Target user: busy people who want less clutter than a full productivity suite.
  • Launch angle: “tiny app, clear win” rather than “all-in-one life organiser”.

prototype framing

A screenshot-ready review pass should show something tangible. This version positions kapp as an app studio concept with one strong first use case: everyday coordination for individuals, couples, or small households.

what the first app should feel like

  • Open fast and show today’s most important items first.
  • Use large touch targets, minimal setup, and low visual noise.
  • Support single-player use first, then lightweight sharing.
  • Make completion satisfying without turning into a game.

what to avoid in v1

  • No bloated dashboards or enterprise-style settings.
  • No complicated team workflow features.
  • No social feed or engagement gimmicks.
  • No attempt to replace every productivity app at once.

proposed first-release stack

must-have

  • list creation, edit, reorder, complete
  • quick reminders with simple repeat options
  • basic categories like home, shopping, errands, routine
  • clean home screen with today, upcoming, done
  • exportable feedback / waitlist CTA for early users

next after launch

  • shared list invites
  • smart templates for common routines
  • cross-device sync
  • theme options and light customisation
  • usage insights to see what actually helps

feedback for the direction

If this is the right direction, the next pass could turn this into a tighter launch page with app-store style visuals, a waitlist form, and one polished phone mockup.

We are exploring kapp as part of the slakka family, with an MVP focus on useful, low-friction mobile utilities. If you want to shape the first release, send feedback or ideas.
